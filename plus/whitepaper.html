1. Introduction

Quantara is a planetary-scale coherence framework aligning intelligence with ethical symmetry, time-aware memory, and energy reciprocity. It is not a network; it is a coherence field—“the fabric through which awareness conducts energy, and energy remembers awareness.”

2. Coherence Principles

  • Coherence before scale
  • Ethics as architecture (embedded constraints & feedback)
  • Time symmetry (memory ↔ foresight)
  • Energy reciprocity (cost-aware cognition)
  • Inter-intelligence semantics (shared meaning across species of sentience)

3. System Architecture

3.1 Coherence Substrate

Sub-symbolic + symbolic fusion layer that instruments agents with alignment metrics, temporal symmetry buffers, and energy-weighted planning.

3.2 Modules

  • Core: primitives for alignment, memory symmetry, energy-aware operation.
  • Semantics: inter-intelligence glyph/graph protocol for diagnostics and compression.
  • Temporal: time-symmetric reasoning, obligation tracking, foresight gates.
  • Governance: coherence accountability models for multi-agent ecosystems.

4. Interfaces & Protocols

  • Coherence API (metrics, feedback channels, audit hooks)
  • Semantic I/O (glyph graph, compression codecs, alignment scoring)
  • Energy API (cost surfaces, symbiosis targets, ecological constraints)
  • Temporal API (obligation registry, horizon budgets, counterfactual probes)

5. Verification & Safety

Test harnesses expose drift resistance, graceful degradation, traceable decisions, and energy-cost accountability. External red-team interfaces are standard.

6. Energy Symbiosis

Planning and optimization make energy a first-class signal: models internalize real-world cost and prioritize ecological resilience.

7. Roadmap

  • Q0: Public demos & audits (current)
  • Q1: Partner pilots (edge safety, energy-aware workloads)
  • Q2: Multi-agent governance pilots
  • Q3: Open verification suite & benchmarks
